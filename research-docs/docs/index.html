Here you can find list of modules ready to be used that come out of the box with Internet Server Platform.

They are provided to satisfy communication needs for people in all areas as a basic requirement.
Based on these services, ISP encurages people on taking on the next level in their academic researches,
 by not making them worry about things done billiions of times again and again.
The ISP is designed also to be used by researchers in their activities. As a proof for this, ISP was engaged in CM.

Also these modules show and encurage a clever and easy way to develop
complex yet easy to make applications. One of the means for this being Trolltech's Qt.
